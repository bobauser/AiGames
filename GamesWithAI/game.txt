wordle:

AI vs Human
You decide a random length (between 3-10 characters for example) and then AI creates a word, Both player and AI is allowed to guess,
and both has their own guess counters, and will race to guess the word

VS Human
AI creates a word, and human will try to answer. Human can decide the settings of how long, and how difficult the word should be,

VS Ai
Ai tries to guess your word, you will give the AI clues
